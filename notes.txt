A multi-sig smart contract designed to disperse funds upon a crypto-hodler's death.

Full delegates: additional keys held by the owner.
Beneficiary delegates: keys held by the beneficiaries of the contract.

1) Create private key that can withdraw funds.
  - Private key can only withdraw after a certain number of blocks of inactivity.

Unlock functionality:
  Beneficiary requests the wallet unlock, waits a specefied amount of time, if the owner does not decline, the wallet is unlocked to all beneficiaries.

Users:
- Parents
- Non-crypto significat others / spouses
- Crypto-nerd friends
